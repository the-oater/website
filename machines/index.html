Production

With our machine, you can produce up to 5 Liters per batch. This ensures that you don’t run out of oat drink in your peak demand periods. The batch production takes around 80 minutes. You can follow the progress of the production on the screen.

Dispensing

Once the oat drink is ready, you simply dispense it at the push of a button. We supply bottles that fit the machine dimensions. We recommend letting the oat drink rest for about 15 minutes and then storing it in the refrigerator since it’s a fresh product.

Cleaning

The Oater Barista requires no manual cleaning. After dispensing, you install the cleaning shield, and the machine automatically starts a thorough cleaning cycle. This process takes 15 minutes and has been lab-tested for hygiene.

Fresh Oat Drink, No Hassle

Installation

We get you started with The Oater. Our team handles the installation of our machine at your business. There are only a few requirements you need to meet before we get started. You need to ensure there is:
- Sufficient counterspace (please consult the dimensions below)
- Access to an electrical socket (not an extension socket)
- Access to the fixed water supply.

We can’t wait to bring The Oater Barista to your business!

Maintenance

Maintenance and Support are part of your subscription. This includes email and phone assistance, video diagnostic within 24h, as well as the repair or the replacement of the machine or parts. It’s important to us that you feel supported with this new addition to your business.
